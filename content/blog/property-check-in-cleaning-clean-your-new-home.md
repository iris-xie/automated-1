**Lease agreement signed, keys in hand, excited to start a new life! But before moving things into the apartment, we need to clean it thoroughly. After all, no one wants to live in a place filled with dust and bacteria. So, check-in cleaning is an essential step that shouldn't be overlooked.**

2.1.1 Preparation for Cleaning Tools [​](https://zufang.ababtools.com/s5#_2-1-1-%E5%87%86%E5%A4%87%E6%B8%85%E6%B4%81%E5%B7%A5%E5%85%B7)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**The first step in achieving cleanliness is to prepare the necessary tools and equipment, just like soldiers standing at attention before a battle. Before starting the cleaning process, gather all cleaning supplies and materials, as you would do for a mission-critical operation.**

*   Gloves: Protect your hands from cleaning agents with rubber gloves.
*   cloths: Wipe away dust and stains.
*   Cleaning products: Choose appropriate cleaning agents such as 84 disinfecting liquid, alcohol-based cleaner, kitchen cleaner, bathroom cleaner, etc., depending on the**Floor is a place we interact with every day, and cleanliness must be maintained.**

*Cleaning steps:  
a) First, use broom or vacuum cleaner to sweep up dust on the floor.  
b) According to the type of floor, choose appropriate cleaning agent, then use sponge to mop the floor.  
c) For wooden floors, pay attention not to use wet sponge, otherwise it will damage the floor.

**Important tips:**

*When performing deep cleaning, wear protective gloves to protect your hands.
*Be careful when using cleaning agents to avoid contact with eyes and skin.
*During cleaning process, keep the room well-ventilated to avoid inhaling harmful gases.
*Regular cleaning is necessary to maintain a clean and comfortable living environment.

Through the above steps, you should be able to clean the rental unit thoroughly and safely. Good housekeeping during your stay will ensure a peaceful living without any worries. We hope this cleaning guide can help you create a clean and comfortable home for yourself. By transforming this small rental unit into your own cozy little nest.
