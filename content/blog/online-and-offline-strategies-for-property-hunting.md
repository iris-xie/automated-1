The information is accurate and trustworthy, making them your first choice. Cities like Nanjing, Hangzhou, and Hefei have housing guarantees and property management agencies. You can simply search for them to find the properties.

* Microphone super talk: Search for "city name" + "renting" on Weibo to find local renting super talks. You'll find a lot of property information there by chatting with roommates or looking at ads.
