2.3.3 Utility Fees: Record payment details, manage usage reasonably [](https://zufang.ababtools.com/s7#_2-3-3-%E6%B0%B4%E7%94%B5%E7%87%83%E6%B0%94%E8%B4%B9-%E8%AE%B0%E5%BD%95%E7%BC%B4%E7%BA%B3%E6%83%85%E5%86%B5-%E5%90%88%E7%90%86%E4%BD%BF%E7%94%A8)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Utility fees, such as water, electricity, and gas bills, are essential monthly expenses in your rental life. It is important to understand the billing methods and payment schedules in the local area to avoid unnecessary inconveniences.

1. Payment Methods:  
Now, utility bills can be paid online through platforms like WeChat or Alipay, or you can hand-deliver the bill to designated collection points at the service center.

2. Payment Deadlines:  
- *Maintenance Fees:* Typically due upon signing the lease agreement. If there is a waiver of maintenance fees, there is no need to worry about this.  
- *Gas Fees:* Usually paid quarterly. You can pay online or visit the service center for both online and offline payment options.  
- *Electricity Fees:* Typically billed monthly, with account statements arriving between the 1st and the 10th of each month. The exact billing date may vary locally; please check with your electricity company or customer service.

3. Important Notes:  
- Always keep a copy of your utility bill after payment to use as a reference for future inquiries.  
- Manage water, electricity, and gas usage responsibly to save energy and reduce living costs.  
- Regularly inspect and maintain water, electricity, and gas systems to address any issues promptly and avoid potential safety hazards.
