Rental is like battling monsters while level-ing up. Having gone through this journey, we have learned a lot and compiled some experiences to share with fellow workers, hoping everyone can find an ideal living space and enjoy a peaceful life.

3.3.1 Pitfalls we fell into in the past [​](https://zufang.ababtools.com/s11#_3-3-1-%E9%82%A3%E4%BA%9B%E5%B9%B4-%E6%88%91%E4%BB%AC%E8%B8%A9%E8%BF%87%E7%9A%84%E5%9D%91)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Don’t just look at the price; consider location, transportation, environment, and facility conditions.  
*   Be cautious of false ads that may be traps with beautiful photos and low prices.  
*   Beware of unlicensed landlords who rent without authorization; this poses a high risk.  
*   Don’t blindly believe  advice; conduct on-site inspections and compare multiple options.  
*   Read the lease agreement carefully before signing, paying particular attention to rent, security deposit, and repair responsibilities.  
*   Maintain good communication with landlords and roommates to avoid conflicts and potential misunderstandings.  
*   Always be cautious regarding safety; ensure fire and theft prevention measures are taken seriously.
