Renting is like a spiritual practice for many youngsters in cities. Face the high home prices, crowded traffic, and renting has become an unavoidable yet necessary choice. However, renting is not as difficult nor as simple as it seems. You might imagine having a cozy small home filled with your favorite things, living freely, but reality often brings you into traps, making it difficult.

This  is like a workbook for newbies learning how to survive in the city by renting. It provides essential knowledge and helps you find an ideal home.
